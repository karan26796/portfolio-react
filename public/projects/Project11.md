### 🧩 The problem
#### Managing holiday calendars across multiple locations and regional offices caused confusion and administrative overhead.

</section>

<section>

### 📅 Part I: Legacy Calendar Constraints
#### The old system assumed a single holiday list for all employees, forcing admins to create manual workarounds for regional branches.

I analyzed customer feedback and HR workflows to identify the friction points in holiday configuration.

#### 🔴 What didn't work
- **Single location lock** — Companies with offices across different states couldn't assign region-specific holidays easily.
- **Cluttered interface** — Admins had to wade through nested menus just to edit a single holiday date.

</section>

### 🔄 Multi-Location & Flexible Grouping
I redesigned the framework so admins can create holiday groups based on location, department, and custom policies.

</section>

<section>

### ⏸️ Part II: Pause & Exception Rules
#### Admins needed the ability to temporarily pause holiday rules or configure ad-hoc holiday additions/deletions without breaking payroll logic.

We introduced quick actions for adding, deleting, and pausing holiday rules per location.

</section>

<section>

### 💚 Results & Key Learnings

1. **Multi-Location Support** — Enabled regional holiday lists for enterprise teams across various branches.
2. **Simplified Exception Handling** — Reduced HR support tickets related to incorrect holiday application by 70%.
3. **Faster Setup** — Reduced time required for annual holiday setup from hours to a few minutes.
