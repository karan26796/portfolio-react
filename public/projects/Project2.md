### The problem
#### The old highlights view became outdated with the introduction of **project structures** and **question scripts** in Looppanel.

Although this gave visibility across calls, it was hard to make sense of the data.

</section>

<img src="/project-imgs/looppanel-insights/iteration-mid.gif" caption="The old highlights view — a flat list with no way to group or make sense of the data."></img>

<section>

### Research & insights

We looked at the tools researchers already used — **Figjam, Excel, and Dovetail** — and realized they constantly **zoomed in and out** of their data to make sense of it.

#### Key findings
1. Researchers **tagged notes during each call**, then zoomed out to see patterns across all calls, then zoomed back in to dig into specifics.
2. **Sharing findings was easy** because stakeholders used the same tools — no friction.
3. **Stakeholder buy-in was easier** when insights were backed by evidence — a stakeholder could see the exact notes that led to a conclusion.

</section>

<img src="/project-imgs/looppanel-insights/competitors.webp" caption="Figjam, Excel, and Dovetail all let researchers move between detail levels — a pattern we used as our north star."></img>

<section>

### Competitor analysis

1. **Excel** gave users a tabular view where they could tag, see data in a cellular format, and customize it heavily.
2. **Figjam** let users color, tag, and move sticky notes on an infinite canvas.
3. **Dovetail** let users switch between views — table and canvas — where they could add tags and further group data.

</section>

<section>

### The solution

With a small team, building a sticky-note canvas was too risky given the **tech constraints**. So I explored options that could **show data across all calls in a project** while still letting researchers **zoom in and out** of their data.

</section>

### Final solution

We landed on **collapsible sections** for each question in the script, with that question's highlights nested below — letting researchers **dive deep into a single question** without losing sight of the broader project context.

</section>

<img src="/project-imgs/looppanel-insights/new-insights.gif" caption="Final design: Collapsible sections per question, with all tagged highlights nested below each one."></img>

### Results & key learnings

1. **Strategic scaling** — the system supported the introduction of complex project structures without overwhelming existing users.
2. **Velocity of discovery** — collapsible views and script-based navigation significantly reduced the time required to group and categorize insights.
3. **Stakeholder buy-in** — the summary view became a critical tool for researchers to demonstrate value to cross-functional teams.

</section>
