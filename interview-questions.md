# Interview Questions & Answers Template

## 🎨 For Designers & Hiring Managers

### Work Experience
7 years across b2b, b2c

### Educational qualifications
Bachelor's in Engineering and Master's in Design from NID

### Q: Can you walk me through your end-to-end design process?
**A:** The process evolves as per the project. If it's a big project, I usually research thoroughly, understand competitors, talk to customers, and then start designing. Otherwise, a quick AI prototype to align the stakeholders is good enough before presenting it to the users.

### Q: How do you balance user needs with hard business goals?
**A:** I work closely with the business stakeholders throughout the process & spend enough time to refine the designs so they follow the design principles.

### Q: How do you handle disagreements with Product Managers or Engineers?
**A:** I usually back the conversations with data and customer feedback, which reframes the problem from a you or me debate to the customer's perspective.

### Q: Which of your portfolio projects was the most challenging and why?
**A:** The most challenging project was creating Looppanel's Highlight view for bulk tagging and summary of research notes. It had a lot of tech constraints and first principles thinking involved.

### Q: How do you measure the success of a design after it launches?
**A:** Success criteria is usually set before the project starts. I usually look at customer tickets, talk to the sales team, and check the usage metrics to measure the success of a design.

### Q: What design tools (Figma, Framer, etc.) and prototyping workflows are you most proficient in?
**A:** I am a Figma trainer, and use Claude, Figma make, & Google AI studio for prototypes.

### Q: Can you give an example of a time you used data to inform a design decision?
**A:** I looked at usage trends on Keka wall which was falling and decided to use my intuition, competitor analysis to create a simple Wish CTA that boosted the engagement 5x.
